Spotting AI Phishing Attempts

Phishing attacks have evolved rapidly in recent years. With the rise of artificial intelligence, scammers can now create highly convincing emails that mimic real companies and even personal writing styles.


These AI-generated messages are harder to detect, making it more important than ever to stay alert and protect your personal information.


🤖 What is AI Phishing?

AI phishing uses advanced tools to generate realistic emails that look legitimate. These messages often imitate trusted brands, services, or even people you know.


🚨 Common Signs to Watch

  • ⚠️ Urgent messages asking for quick action
  • ⚠️ Slightly unusual email addresses
  • ⚠️ Links that don't match the official website
  • ⚠️ Requests for sensitive information

🛡️ How to Protect Yourself

Always double-check links before clicking, avoid sharing personal information, and verify the sender's identity when something feels suspicious.

Using a temporary email can also reduce your exposure to phishing attempts by keeping your primary inbox private.
