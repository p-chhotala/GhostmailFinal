What is Email Tracking & How to Stop It

Email tracking is a technique used by companies to monitor your activity when you open emails. Most tracking is done using invisible pixels embedded inside the message.

When you open a tracked email, it can reveal your location, device, and even the time you opened it. This data is often used for marketing and behavioral profiling.

🕵️ How Tracking Works

A tiny hidden image loads when you open an email. This sends a request back to the sender, confirming that the email was opened.

🛡️ How to Avoid It

  • ✔ Disable automatic image loading
  • ✔ Avoid clicking unknown links
  • ✔ Use temporary email addresses
