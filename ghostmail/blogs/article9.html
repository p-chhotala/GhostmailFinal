Why You Should Not Share Your Email Publicly

Sharing your email address publicly may seem harmless, but it opens the door to a wide range of risks. Bots constantly scan the internet for exposed email addresses.

⚠️ Risks of Public Exposure

Once your email is collected, it can be added to spam lists, sold to advertisers, or targeted in phishing attacks.
