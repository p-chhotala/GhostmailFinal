About GhostMail

GhostMail is a free temporary email service designed for speed, privacy, and simplicity.

It allows you to generate a disposable email instantly and receive messages without signing up or sharing personal information.


In today's internet, your email is constantly exposed — from signups to downloads to random websites. GhostMail helps you stay protected by giving you a temporary inbox that keeps your real email safe from spam, tracking, and unwanted messages.


No accounts. No tracking. No complications. Just a working inbox when you need it.

How to Use Temp-Mail

Generate

Visit a temporary email website and generate one.

Copy

Copy the generated email and use it wherever an email is required.

Receive mail (OTP)

it will appear in your temporary inbox.

Discard

When you no longer need the email, simply leave the page.
